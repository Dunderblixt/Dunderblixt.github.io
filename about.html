Bio

Born 1987 in Sweden to finnish parents. He initially started out studying engineering at Lund university, but another interest took precedent: Music. While studying he befriended a DJ who taught him how to mix, which set him on a new path. After a sabatical financed by DJing, he went back to uni and switched subjects, taking musicology and music production. After a few years of tinkering and honing his craft, he released his first EP independently in 2019. He followed with two singles within a year, and in 2022 he released his debut album Metalworks: Sounds of Steel Vol. 1.
